### Edit format brief introduction

1. "# " means h1
1. "## " means h2
1. "### " means h3
1. "\*\*content\*\*" means bold
1. "\_content\_" means italic
1. "\`content\`" means code
1. "* " means unordered list
1. "1. " means ordered list
1. "> " means blockquote
1. "***" means horizontal rule
